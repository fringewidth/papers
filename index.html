Unpacking My Research for Everyone

Academic papers follow a specific format for good reasons: rigor, reproducibility, peer review. But that same format can make fascinating research harder to engage with, especially when you're just trying to understand the core ideas.

I've always appreciated when researchers take the time to share their work in more accessible ways alongside the formal publications. So I figured I'd try doing that with my own research.

This site is where I break down my work into more digestible pieces. Sometimes that's explaining the intuition behind an algorithm. Sometimes it's walking through a mathematical proof with more context. Sometimes it's highlighting the one insight that makes everything click. Whatever helps bridge the gap between a formal paper and understanding the actual ideas.
